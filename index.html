Long Haul

Designed for long-distance public transit commuters, this app takes in custom route data (selected by the user), updates current travel and ETA times for a specific route, and alerts commuters 5 minutes before arrival at a desingated stop. The best thing is, it updates by the minute with real-time data to ensure accurate arrival times. Finally, you can fall asleep on the bus with confidence!
